About Craft Club

What can I do at Craft Club?

Painting & Drawing

There are many types of painting and drawing activities that kids can enjoy, such as watercolor painting, acrylic painting, finger painting, and sketching. They can also explore different styles and themes, such as landscapes, animals, and abstract art. With the right materials and guidance, painting and drawing can be a wonderful way for kids to express themselves and have fun!

Crafty Kids

At our craft club, kids can expect to engage in a variety of exciting and hands-on activities, such as, decopatch, mosaics, sculpting, felting, making bath bombs, bumpy doodle glazes, quilling, and more. Our experienced instructors will guide your child through each project and provide them with the tools and materials they need to bring their ideas to life. This is the perfect place for kids to express themselves and have fun. They'll learn new skills, develop their imagination, and make memories that will last a lifetime.

Clay Play

Children will learn different pottery techniques and materials, such as clay, glazes, and firing methods. Start with pouring moulds, carefully remove the piece and begin shaping and refining it with tools and their own creativity. They will then decorate and glaze their pottery. At the end of the class, each child will have their own finished pottery piece that they can take home and proudly display.

Create and Celebrate

Make your child's next birthday party a creative and memorable experience with our kids craft club parties! We can also provide decorations, party favors, and refreshments to make the occasion extra special. So why wait? Book a kids craft club party today and give your child a birthday celebration they'll never forget!
