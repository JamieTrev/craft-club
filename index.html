About Craft Club

What can I do at Craft Club?

Painting & Drawing

Here at Craft Club we use a selection of different paints and techniques such as watercolor painting, acrylic painting, finger painting, and sketching. We explore different styles, such as landscapes, animals, and abstract art.

Crafty Kids

At Craft Club, kids can expect to engage in a variety of hands-on activities, such as, decopatch, mosaics, sculpting, felting, bumpy doodle glazes, quilling, making bath bombs and more. This is the perfect place for kids to express themselves and have fun.

Clay Play

We teach different pottery techniques and materials, such as clay, glazes, and firing methods. Start with pouring moulds, carefully remove and begin shaping and refining it with tools and their own creativity. They will then decorate and glaze their pottery. Each child will have their own finished pottery piece that they can take home and proudly display.

Create and Celebrate

Make your child's next birthday party a creative and memorable experience with our kids craft club parties! We can also provide decorations, party favors, and refreshments to make the occasion extra special. So why wait? Book a kids craft club party today and give your child a birthday celebration they'll never forget!
